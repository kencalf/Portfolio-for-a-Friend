About Me

I am an aspiring Hip-Hop/Alternative artist from Bear Delaware and founder of "Normal Music" working with producers and artists from Delaware, Pennsylvania, New York, California, and FLorida. Having been intrested in music and visual arts since an early age, I started rapping at age 11 with my cousin who also happened to be my neighbor and an avid fan of music as well. I later attended an arts school for visual arts and there found similarly minded people to help refine my talents in music and music production. Since then I have been locally creating a name for myself and giving other talented individuals the opportunity to express themselves.
